Recloy Privacy Policy

Description

Recloy collects SMS information to process your transactional SMS automatically into your expenses. This helps you to manage your finances without spending time on manual tracking No personal SMS are processed. This information is never shared to any third party.

SMS Permission

To proccess your expenses, we need information your sms, so Recloy needs this permission to read sms Recloy assures you that your privacy will be respected and your personal messages will not be processed and your transactions will not be shared with third parties.

BIOMETRIC Permission

To safe information and your privacy, you must enter the security lock you registered for your device when logging into the app. Recloy does not save your security lock information, so you must enter your security lock every time you log into the app.

INTERNET Permission

To process expenses, your transaction information is encrypted and sent to Recloy's secure servers, and the result is sent to you.
